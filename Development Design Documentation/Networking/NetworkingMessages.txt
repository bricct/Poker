

Commands/Messages:

From client to server:

'client ID':fold
current client folds

'client ID':check
current client checks

'client ID':raise
current client raises


From Server to client:

'Server':'client ID':card number
Server sends a client their cards 


'Server':'client ID':fold
Another client has folded


'Server':'client ID':check
Another client has checked 


'Server':'client ID':raise
Another client has raised
